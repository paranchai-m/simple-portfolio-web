# Paranchai Malaikanok Portfolio

## Palynologist | Fossil Pollen Specialist (Southeast Asia)

A professional portfolio website for **Paranchai Malaikanok**, showcasing research highlights, publications, and professional certifications. The site features a **Neo-minimalist Bauhaus** aesthetic—fusing authoritative modernism with interactive digital elements.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy: Neo-minimalist Bauhaus
The project follows a high-contrast, geometric design system inspired by the Bauhaus movement, enhanced with "Neo-Minimal" floating components.
- **Typography:** Bold geometric headings in **Space Grotesk** paired with highly readable **Inter** body text.
- **Palette:** Bauhaus Red (`#E3120B`), Stark Black (`#000000`), and Crisp White.
- **Interactivity:** Content blocks use scroll-triggered reveal animations and interactive hover states for a "living" document feel.

## 🔬 Research Highlights
Specializing in the palynological analysis of fossil pollen to reconstruct ancient environments in Thailand and Southeast Asia:
- **Ericales Diversity (2025):** Evidence of stratified mountain forests in the late Oligocene.
- **Fagaceae Community Assembly (2023):** Tracking tropical oak forests back to the Eocene.
- **Gnetum Morphology (2018):** Research on harmomegathic characters in Thai Gnetum species.
- **Kho Hong Hill Flora:** Survey of gymnosperm and monocot pollen diversity.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Structure:** Semantic HTML5
- **Styling:** CSS3 (Custom properties, Grid/Flexbox layouts, Intersection Observer animations)
- **Scripting:** Vanilla JavaScript
- **Fonts:** Space Grotesk & Inter (via Google Fonts)

## 📁 Directory Structure
- `index.html`: Main landing page with Publications and Certifications.
- `style.css`: Core design system and layout.
- `script.js`: Interactive reveal logic.
- `my_profile/`: Repository for PDFs, JSON credentials, and research assets.
- `plan.md`: Development roadmap and design iterations.
